\section{Experimental Setup}
\label{sec:methodology}

\paragraph{Study 1: 2D synthetic distributions.} Four flow-matching variants (OT-CFM, VP-CFM, Target-CFM, SB-CFM) are compared on \texttt{checkerboard}, \texttt{two\_moons}, and \texttt{gaussian\_mixture}. The velocity field is a time-conditioned MLP (3 layers, 256 units, SiLU). Quality is measured via SWD and MMD at NFE=64, with additional trajectory curvature and NFE-robustness diagnostics.

\paragraph{Study 2: CIFAR-10 factorial ablation.} The three variance-reduction axes are crossed in a full $2^3$ design (Table~\ref{tab:cells}). All 8 cells share a U-Net backbone (${\sim}9$M parameters, base channels 64, attention at $16{\times}16$), trained with Adam ($\text{lr}{=}10^{-4}$), EMA decay 0.9999, fp16 mixed precision, batch size 256, for 80k steps on CIFAR-10. Training ran on AMD MI210 GPUs. FID \citep{heusel2017gans} is evaluated at NFE $\in \{5,10,20,40,100\}$ with Euler, midpoint, and RK4 solvers (15 evaluations per cell). Cells that diverged to NaN used a pre-divergence checkpoint (step 10k) for evaluation.
